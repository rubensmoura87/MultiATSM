# Data: domestic risk factors - Candelon and Moura (2024, JFEC)

Domestic risk factors data used in Candelon and Moura (2024, JFEC)

## Usage

``` r
data("DomMacro")
```

## Format

A matrix of country-specific risk factors (inflation and economic
activity growth) for Brazil, China, Mexico, and Uruguay. The data have
monthly frequency and span the period from June/2004 to January/2020.

## Source

- Inflation:

  year-over-year variation from Consumer price index published by the
  International Monetary Fund
  \<https://data.imf.org/en/datasets/IMF.STA:CPI\>

- Eonomic activity:

  GDP leading indicator published by the OECD
  \<https://www.oecd.org/en/data/indicators/composite-leading-indicator-cli.html\>

## References

Candelon, B. and Moura, R. (2024) "A Multicountry Model of the Term
Structures of Interest Rates with a GVAR". (Journal of Financial
Econometrics)
